Frequently Asked Questions

What people usually ask before deciding

Is this suitable for someone with limited mobility?
It depends on the program and your mobility level. Tell us your specific situation before booking and we design accordingly. We don't use "suitable for everyone" as a slogan.
What makes this different from a traditional tour?
In a traditional tour, the itinerary serves the logistics. In ours, logistics serve the human contact. We spend real time with families, artisans, and community leaders in their actual spaces. Groups of 4 to 10, no 40-person buses.
Do I need to speak Spanish?
No. Facilitators are bilingual (Spanish/English). That said, basic Spanish makes direct access to communities naturally richer. Not required, but worth trying.
What's included in the price?
Accommodation, internal transport in Colombia, meals per itinerary, facilitators, community and cultural site access, and a direct contribution to local initiatives. International flights and travel insurance not included. Full breakdown shared before confirmation.
How physically demanding are the experiences?
Moderate baseline. Walks of 30 minutes to 2 hours on variable terrain. Nothing requiring special training. Days include real free time. If you have specific conditions, tell us and we adjust.
Can I travel solo?
Yes — a significant part of our travelers do. Small groups naturally facilitate connection between people. You're not alone operationally, but your experience is entirely your own.
